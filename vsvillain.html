VillainS

"VillainS" (also referred to as "Versus villainS" or "VS") is the 9th mini album by Dreamcatcher. It was released on November 22, 2023 with "OOTD" serving as the title track. The physical album features 12 versions in total: Limited "C" version, Normal "U", "R", "S", "E" versions and the Poca Albums that comes in 7 versions, one for each member. This is the first installment of the VersuS Series.


Background and Release

The account @ymenehcra was created in September 2023, and started posting on October 12 of the same year, among the cryptic posts and images, one is a letter with instructions.

Fans also found out that there is a Youtube channel with the same name, which has a profile picture of a crown that bears some resemblance to Celly's face, and who has been leaving comments on all the Dreamcatcher MVs and related videos, always talking about a crown.

According with @ymehehcra99, they were hanging out with friends before they found a mystery letter, after that they started to have severe headaches and little fragments of memories appeared one after the other, the letter said that whoever picked up was in danger and they had to find a crown, which led them to an obsession and fixation to find the crown, the hashtag #FindTheCrown was created with the help of insomnia to find it.

October 13, 2023 Dreamcatcher posted on Twitter the comeback schedule for their 9th mini album, "VillainS".
